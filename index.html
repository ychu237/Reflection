Re::flection
This project aims to explore the philosophy of machine language and the nature of human life.

ME:thod
Use different punctuation marks to compose the received images. The user can zoom in and out of these punctuation marks with the mouse position.

IM:mortality
If human consciousness could be stored permanently on the cloud by means of a device, what would it look like? Is the consciousness in the machine the same person as the real one, or is it just a fragment of the consciousness that remains?
